How to Investigate a Sanctioned Oligarch

In our 2017 game, Kleptocrat, we showed how corrupt public officials made and hid their dirty money.

The patterns we found in our research and implemented into the game still hold true today, but with some new wrinkles.

It all starts with a bribe, a shady deal, or a kickback scheme.

Banks and lawyers can help create structures, shell companies, and other lock boxes to hide dirty money.

In the digital age, money moves through wires, but physical assets can have their advantages.

Usually, clever spouses, siblings, childhood friends, and children are instrumental to hiding dirty money.

Sometimes attention needs to be drawn away, evidence concealed, traces hidden.

Dirty money likes to come out and play. Shopping sprees, yachts, mansions, etc.
